YMCA Website User Manual

Getting Started

If you are currently looking at our Welcome screen, press the Sign In / Register button in the top right-hand corner to browse and sign up for classes.

If you already have an account, enter your username and password on the Sign In screen. If not, click the small text that says "Sign up here" to create a YMCA account.

Creating an Account

From the registration page, enter all required information including a secure password and your mailing address (used to send fliers).

At the "Membership Type" selection:

  • Member: Requires a monthly fee, but includes discounted classes and access to special YMCA areas.
  • Nonmember: No monthly fee, but class prices are higher.

When you're ready, click the "Create Account" button (left side). You’ll be redirected to the Browse Classes page.

Browsing and Registering for Classes

On the Browse Classes page, you’ll see all available upcoming classes. Read descriptions carefully — some may list prerequisites.

Note: The YMCA does not enforce class leveling, but we encourage you to consider your experience and ability before registering.

To register, click the blue "Register" button next to the desired class. If the button is grayed out, the class is either full or cancelled.

After clicking "Register", you'll be shown a confirmation screen with the class time, location, and prices (member vs nonmember). Click "Confirm Registration" to finalize your sign-up.

Important: Billing is handled in person at the YMCA front desk. A receptionist will assist you with payment at that time.

Managing Your Classes

After registering, click "My Classes" from the top navigation bar (next to "Help") to view your upcoming classes.

To cancel a class, click the class entry from the My Classes page, then choose "Unregister".

While we encourage continued participation, we understand conflicts arise. We appreciate your communication.

Family Accounts

From the navigation bar, you can click "Family Account" to view your linked family members and shared account info.

Note: You cannot create, add, or delete family members unless logged in with a staff account.

Logging Out

When finished, click the "Logout" button in the top right, then close the browser window.

Thank You

Thank you for using the YMCA website. We’re proud to have you as part of our community — working together for a healthier tomorrow.
